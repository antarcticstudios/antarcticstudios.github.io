About

A short History

Antarctic Studios was founded in early 2020, with the vision to help new and upcoming artists create polished sounding, radio-ready music at an affordable price. With Harrison mixing console technology at our fingertips, you can be sure to get the warm analog sound that you can find on golden records like Thriller at Antarctic Studios.

Where?

Currently located in Kitchener, Ontario, Antarctic Studios is still relatively new, and as such, the studio is still being built. Due to this and the current pandemic, we are not open for in-person studio work, but are open for virtual collaboration work. Rates are available on our session rates page .

Portfolio

Although only recently started, Antarctic Studios has a few pieces of music available online, with more on the way. Have a listen to our portfolio in our Portfolio section.
