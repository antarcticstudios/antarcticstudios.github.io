Studio Policies & FAQ

Deposits

A deposit is required to secure your booking. The amount varies depending on the type and length of the session. If a deposit is not received within 72 hours, the booking may be cancelled and the time slot made available to other clients. Deposits are non-refundable. If you need to cancel, the deposit can be applied toward a future booking or service. If the cancellation is on our end, your deposit will be fully refunded.


Payment methods

E-transfer is the easiest method - cash is also accepted, along with Paypal and credit card online via Quickbooks invoices. We do not have a physical card reader at the studio.


In-Person Sessions

Sessions begin at the agreed-upon time. If you arrive late, the session will still end at the scheduled time and the rate will not be adjusted. With at least 48 hours’ notice, we may be able to adjust your start time depending on availability.
To help make the most of your session, we recommend sending any files you’d like to work on (such as stems or demos) at least 24 hours in advance so we can prepare.
The studio does not operate in Pro Tools. If you would like to work from a Pro Tools session, please send stems or a demo for us to work from instead.
You are welcome to use any of the studio equipment during your session. Please refer to the gear list, and feel free to ask if you have questions about available equipment. While most gear is usually available, occasional servicing may make certain items temporarily unavailable, so please check in advance if you plan to use something specific.


Mixing and Mastering Services

All mixing and mastering services include three additional revisions after the first mix delivery.
